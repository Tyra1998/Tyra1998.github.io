    Awards/Achievements

    Booker T. Washington Award Of Leadership - Startup Hub (Summer of 2015)

    I attended AgriTrek/SciTrek in the summer of 2015. This program lasted two weeks.The main motive behind the project was to learn more about agriculture. My current high school is agriculture driven. Piney Woods influenced me to join MANRRS and even sign up for the program in Tuskegee, Alabama.     Presidential Scholar- The Piney Woods School (2015-2016)

    My memories at Piney Woods are priceless. They not only include my extra curriculum activities, but also include academic excellence. My sophmore year was all about adapting and getting use to a new enviornment. I was an honor student for sure, but I made a few Bs. However, my junior year was full of fantastic accomplishments.
